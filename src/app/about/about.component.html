About

Since human-machine communication is heavily dependent on understanding the machine it is my personal claim to be skilled in the Web Development tools HTML5, SASS, Bootstrap, Javascript, jQuery and Angular as well as C# and VVVV.

If I am not busy with any work-related stuff you usually find me in the climbing gym, riding my bike, cooking for friends or on a skiing trip.
