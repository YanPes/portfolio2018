Electrify Aesthetics

Lighting and Material Study to keep my creative Juices flowing

Introduction

The idea of this small project hit me while rendering a series of shots for an automotive exhibition. It is a lighting and material study to keep my creative juices flowing after some busy extra hour weeks at work.

Made with: Modo, Substance Designer, V-Ray

Result

Conclusion

Although the project was fairly close to my daily work I had a lot of fun fiddling around with the lighting setup and camera positioning. In my opinion it is crucial to keep up the personal skill and software practice momentum during recreation time in order to stay competitive in the industry.
